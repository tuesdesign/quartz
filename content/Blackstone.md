# About

You’re a photographer for a car insurance company. You do the travel brochures. You’ve been tasked with visiting and cataloguing the lovely **Black Stone Island National Park**. _It seems you only have one roll of film, though._

Among the vast sands of time, memories can outlive those who made them.

# Portfolio Note

I created *Blackstone Island National Park* in 48 hours as a part of the Ludum Dare 54 Game Jam. I'm particularly proud of the visual effects and environment.
