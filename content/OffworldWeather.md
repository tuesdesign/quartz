# About

OFFWORLD WEATHER is an upcoming explorative 3D Open World First Person Game for the Game Boy Color. 

You're stationed at Site B on Persephone-1, a barren and lonely planet. What will you find?

**The preview version 'First contact' will release soon! For now, please enjoy a preview of the preview in the form of an interactive map.**

OFFWORLD WEATHER is based on the excellent [famicase entry of the same name](https://famicase.com/23/softs/112.html) by Alec Whitmore.
