# About

Starlight is an Action Roguelite game from Stellar Octopians, set in a story-rich world of magic and mystery. The game blends nostalgia with modern technology with its unique visual style, dynamic soundtrack that adapts to every action, emergent combat customization, and sophisticated procedural level generation. Explore ever-changing dungeons, engaging in frenetic combat and adapting to new and hostile environments. Discover thousands of emergent magical spell combinations, find secrets, and conquer this hostile world.
